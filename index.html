What is Algorand Hodl DAO?

We are a DAO focused on funding a treasury that invests in the Algorand ecosystem, with a focus on promising medium and long-term projects.

Community Governance

Every $AHT owner has voting power to propose and decide on treasury investments.

Full Transparency

Sustainable Growth

Shared Benefits
